Clear Answers

Information designed for search and accurate AI retrieval

What should a homeowner do before requesting a remodeling proposal?

Define the rooms and work included, identify must-have priorities, establish a realistic planning budget, gather available plans or property information, and list the selections or assumptions that remain undecided. A proposal is easier to compare when every contractor is pricing the same scope.

Why do remodel budgets change after demolition?

Demolition can reveal concealed water damage, outdated wiring, deteriorated framing, improper previous work or other conditions that were not visible during the initial inspection. A clearly documented contingency and change-order process helps manage this risk.

How does ADELIE use transparency during planning?

ADELIE focuses on defining scope, assumptions, allowances, exclusions, decision deadlines and changes in clear language so homeowners understand what is included and what could affect price or schedule.
