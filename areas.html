Where We Work

Local knowledge. One consistent standard.

ADELIE Construction serves homeowners throughout North County San Diego and nearby communities. Project availability depends on scope, scheduling, site conditions and permitting requirements.

Whether the work involves one room or the entire property, our approach remains the same: define the scope clearly, communicate honestly and coordinate the work with respect for the home.
