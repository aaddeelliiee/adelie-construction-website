ADELIE Construction — Netlify Forms Setup

1. In Netlify, open Forms and click ENABLE FORM DETECTION.
2. Upload this ZIP with Netlify Drop. Do not extract it.
3. After the deploy is Published, return to Forms. Two forms should appear:
   - project-inquiry
   - ai-lead
4. Open each form and add an Email notification using the email address where you want leads delivered.
5. Submit one test lead from the Contact page and one from Ask ADELIE.
6. Confirm both appear in Netlify Forms and arrive by email.

Important: Email routing is controlled in the Netlify dashboard, not by an email address embedded in the website code.
