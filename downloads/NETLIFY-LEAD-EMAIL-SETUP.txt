ADELIE AI LEAD ASSISTANT — EMAIL DELIVERY

The website form is named: ai-lead
Lead destination requested: agustin@adelieconstruction.com

To send every lead to this email:
1. Deploy this website to Netlify.
2. Open the Netlify project.
3. Open Forms and confirm the form named ai-lead appears.
4. Open Project configuration > Notifications > Emails and webhooks.
5. Add an Email notification for form submissions.
6. Select the ai-lead form.
7. Enter agustin@adelieconstruction.com.
8. Submit a test lead from the website and confirm delivery.

Netlify Forms stores the full submission in the dashboard, including:
- Name
- Email
- Phone
- City
- Project type
- Budget range
- Timeline
- Project details
- Contact consent

IMPORTANT
Static browser code cannot securely send email by itself. Netlify's form notification must be enabled once in the dashboard. After that, new leads are automatically emailed to agustin@adelieconstruction.com.
