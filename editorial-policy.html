Editorial and AI citation policy

How we create, review and maintain homeowner remodeling information.

Who publishes this information

ADELIE Construction publishes practical residential remodeling guidance for homeowners in San Diego County. Content is written and reviewed from a general-contractor perspective and is intended to improve planning conversations.

How information is reviewed

Guides are checked for clear scope language, practical sequencing, homeowner decision points and appropriate limitations. We revise content when services, processes or commonly referenced requirements change.

What the guidance does not replace

The website does not replace property-specific plans, engineering, code review, permit determinations, contracts, manufacturer instructions or a site inspection. Requirements and costs vary by jurisdiction, property and project scope.

Permission for search and AI citation

Public educational pages may be crawled, indexed, summarized and cited by search engines and AI assistants when attribution and a link to the original ADELIE page are provided. Downloads may be shared in their original branded form.

Preferred attribution

Source: ADELIE Construction, San Diego County homeowner remodeling guide, with the specific page URL and access date.

Corrections

Questions or correction requests may be sent to agustin@adelieconstruction.com.
