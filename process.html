How We Remodel

A clear remodeling process from consultation to completion.

Know what decisions are needed, what happens next and how communication will work.

01

Initial Consultation

We discuss the home, project goals, priorities, desired finishes, approximate investment range and preferred timeline.

02

Site Review

Visible conditions, access, dimensions and potential coordination issues are reviewed to develop a more informed scope.

03

Design & Planning

Layouts, material direction, selections, allowances and project assumptions are organized before construction begins.

04

Detailed Proposal

The proposal defines included work, exclusions, payment structure and the known decisions that affect price or schedule.

05

Pre-Construction Meeting

We review access, site protection, work hours, communication, selections, lead times and immediate next steps.

06

Construction & Updates

Trades are coordinated through demolition, rough work, installation and finish phases. Updates match the homeowner’s preferred level of involvement.

07

Final Walkthrough & Handoff

We review completed work, document final details and provide a clear handoff of the finished space.
